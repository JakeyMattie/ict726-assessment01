Dance

Dance has been the most prominent passion that I've ventured in my life.

I started dancing at a really young age. Although that experience wasn't fully professional, I started dancing at school events through simple flash mobs or basic class dancing activities. I immediately fell in love with it when I was immersed into it.

When I entered uni at 2011, that's when I started to learn dance at a more advanced level. I participated in dance crews and competed at my uni. Ever since then I've tried to connect and to attend dance classes to further improve my craft.
